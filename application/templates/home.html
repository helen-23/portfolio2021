About Me

I am a Computer Engineering undergraduate at the University of Toronto (U of T) and an experienced programming tutor. I started coding in the summer of Grade 10 and have since then been very passionate about Computer Science and Engineering as a whole. On this site, you will see some of my selected projects from the past, including single player and multiplayer video games, a chess game AI, and some hardware applications implemented on the FPGA.

During U of T's Professional Experience Year Program (PEY), I interned at Intel San Jose Campus for a full year as a Software Engineering intern. During the summer of 2020, I also did research at U of T with Professor Vaughn Betz. Please check out my resume to see more details about my past academic and professional experiences.

My focuses of study are Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Human-Computer Interaction.
